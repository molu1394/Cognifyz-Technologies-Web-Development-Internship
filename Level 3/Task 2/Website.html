Overview

The Cognifyz Web Development Internship is a structured training program aimed at equipping aspiring developers with real-world experience in building modern, responsive websites and web applications. Interns engage with industry-standard tools and technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and popular frameworks, while working on practical tasks and collaborative projects that mirror real-world development environments. The program encourages innovation, teamwork, and continuous learning under the guidance of experienced mentors. Duration The internship runs for a duration of 4 weeks, providing a compact yet immersive experience. During this time, interns are expected to complete assigned modules, contribute to project work, and participate in regular check-ins or reviews to monitor progress and growth.
